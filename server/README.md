# 🍛 KhanaBook Server: Built for the Heart of the Shop
Hey there! Welcome to the engine room of **KhanaBook**! 👋

We didn't just build a Point of Sale (POS) system; we built a companion for the people who keep our neighborhoods fed. Whether it's the local biryani corner or a bustling breakfast joint, we know that running a small food business is a labor of love, sweat, and early mornings. 

KhanaBook is our way of making that journey a little bit easier.

### 🔌 Why "Offline-First"? Because Life Happens.
We know how it goes—the kitchen is heating up, orders are flying in, and suddenly... the internet goes out. In a busy shop, that shouldn't be a crisis. 

We built KhanaBook to be **offline-first**. It lives on your local network (LAN), so it keeps working even when the Wi-Fi doesn't. When the connection comes back, it quietly syncs everything to the cloud, ensuring every order and every rupee is safely recorded without you ever having to hit "refresh."

---

### ✨ How it Helps Every Day
We focused on the things that actually matter when you're in the thick of it:

*   **No More Guesswork:** Our **Deep Inventory** tracking tells you exactly how much flour or oil is left. You'll know what's in your pantry before you even open the shutters.
*   **Simple, Honest Billing:** GST, variants, and payments are handled in a flow that feels natural, not mechanical. It’s about getting the customer their bill and getting back to the food.
*   **Your Data is Yours:** We use **Secure Multi-Tenancy** to ensure that every restaurant’s data is locked away in its own private vault. Your business secrets are safe with us.
*   **Menu Flexibility:** Changing a price or adding a new seasonal dish should take seconds, not a phone call to tech support. Our management tools are built for speed.

### 🛠️ The Craftsmanship (Tech Stack)
We chose our tools not because they're trendy, but because they are reliable—just like the equipment in a professional kitchen:
*   **Java & Spring Boot:** For a foundation as solid as a cast-iron skillet.
*   **PostgreSQL:** To keep your records safe and organized for years to come.
*   **JWT Security:** Because your trust—and your customers' trust—is everything.

### 🌱 The Vision
Small business owners deserve the same powerful tools that the big chains use, but without the "big tech" headache. We believe software should adapt to the human, not the other way around. 

KhanaBook is built for the "daily grind"—the early prep, the lunch rush, and the satisfied smiles at the end of the day.
